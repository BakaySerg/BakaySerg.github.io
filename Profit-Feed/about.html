Profit feed livestock supplements

NPO Hygiene-Bio LLC, Agrovit LLC is a leading Russian producer of feed and feed additives for farm animals, which has been working steadily on the Russian market for more than 30 years. The holding specializes in the development and production of: feed additives (premixes) for all types of agricultural animals and birds (Ryabushka, Borka, Burenka, etc.); veterinary drugs; vitamins; microfertilizers; preparations for protection of gardens and kitchen gardens from insects; insecticides; plant growth stimulants.

ProfiT feed history

1987-2000 Years
1987

Company estate

LLC "Hygiene-Bio" (founded in 1987). Initially, the company developed and sold vitamin-mineral premixes and protein vitamin-mineral supplements (PMVS) for all types of farm animals and poultry - both for farms and for the private sector. Due to the high quality of products and services, the productivity of animals everywhere increased, especially in cattle. Time dictated new requirements for the qualitative and quantitative composition of feed additives.

1997

The decision

In 1997, the decision was made: to start the development and production of an innovative feed complex to increase the productivity of farm animals. It was the first product of the now popular series Felutsen. In January 1998, together with the Yaroslavl NIIZhIK under the leadership of V.Tanif, a package of scientific and technical documentation for the Felutsen feed complexes was developed and approved.

Today LLC NPO Hygiene-Bio, LLC Agrovit offers its customers a complete line of fodder complexes for dairy and beef cattle, all types of poultry, pigs, herd and sport horses, goats and sheep, and wild animals.
