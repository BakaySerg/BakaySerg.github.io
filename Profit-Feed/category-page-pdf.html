Description

Feed complex to the basic diet of cattle for meat and dairy breeds of all ages, which has a restorative effect on the animal organism. It is a powerful immune modulator, antioxidant and hepatoprotector.

STORAGE CONDITIONS

Store in a cool, dry place with temperature under 25°C and humidity of 75%. Shelf life (if storage conditions are followed) is 9 months from the product manufacture date.

SIDE EFFECTS

This product is made of natural components. It does not contain antibiotics, palm oil, hormones and GMOs. No side effects, contraindications or complications in use of the product. Meat and milk of animals can be used without limitations.

DOSAGE AND METHOD OF APPLICATION

Put briquette (container) in convenient location, providing easy access for animals. Accurate dosing is not required. The average daily consumption depends on the individual needs of the body. Gradual, uniform and regular flow of mix components in the animal organism allows the most complete nutrients absorption. Permanent water access for animals is required.
